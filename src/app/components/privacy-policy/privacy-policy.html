Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 24, 2026. How TRAFFTAG collects, protects, and handles your vehicle & decal data.

1. Commitment to Zero-Identity Disclosure

TRAFFTAG is designed with a fundamental privacy-first architecture. When someone scans your vehicle's QR decal tag, your phone number, real name, email, and personal identity are never revealed to the caller or scanner. All communication is routed through encrypted anonymous gateways.

2. Information We Collect

To provide safety notifications and vehicle decal services, we collect minimal operational information:

  • Account Data: Name, verified email address, phone number, and optional emergency contact numbers.
  • Vehicle Data: Vehicle registration number, make, model, color, and assigned QR decal code.
  • Notification Logs: Timestamp, general alert category (e.g. incorrect parking, emergency assistance), and notification delivery status.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is strictly utilized for core service functionality:

  • Instantly sending SMS, WhatsApp, or voice notifications when your QR decal is scanned.
  • Managing active vehicle subscriptions and QR tag assignments in your portal.
  • Enhancing platform security and preventing fraudulent tag misuse.

We never sell, lease, or monetize your personal phone numbers or vehicle details to third-party advertisers.

4. Data Storage & Security

All sensitive contact data and vehicle records are protected using enterprise-grade AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. System access is restricted strictly to authenticated session tokens with mandatory multi-factor validation.

5. Your Rights & Data Ownership

You maintain 100% control over your registered vehicles and contact settings. Through your TRAFFTAG Portal, you may update, unbind, deactivate, or delete vehicle records and tags at any time with immediate effect.

6. Contact Privacy Team

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or your data protection rights, please reach out to our privacy compliance team at privacy@trafftag.com.
