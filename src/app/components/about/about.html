Who We Are

TRAFFTAG was founded with a singular purpose: to solve the urban vehicle parking and safety dilemma without sacrificing driver privacy. Traditional methods of displaying phone numbers on vehicle dashboards expose car owners to spam, harassment, and identity theft. TRAFFTAG replaces phone numbers with encrypted QR code decals.

How TRAFFTAG Works

Every TRAFFTAG decal contains a unique, tamper-proof QR code linked securely to the vehicle owner's portal. Anyone who scans the tag using a smartphone camera can instantly send a pre-formatted or custom notification—such as an alert for blocked driveways, open windows, lights left on, or emergency situations—without ever seeing the owner's phone number or personal identity.
